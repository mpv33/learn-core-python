# AcmeShop Support Knowledge Base

## Returns & Refunds

- **30-day return window** from delivery date for most items.
- Items must be unused and in original packaging.
- Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** after we receive the return.
- Final sale items (marked at checkout) cannot be returned.
- To start a return, reply with your order ID or visit Account → Orders → Return Item.

## Shipping & Delivery

- **Standard shipping:** 5–7 business days (free over $50).
- **Express shipping:** 2–3 business days ($9.99 flat rate).
- You'll receive a tracking link by email when the order ships.
- If tracking shows **delivered** but you didn't receive the package, wait 48 hours then contact support.

## Billing & Payments

- We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and PayPal.
- Charges appear as **ACME SHOP** on your statement.
- Failed payments: we'll email you within 24 hours; the order is held for 48 hours.
- For duplicate charges, share your order ID and we'll investigate within 2 business days.

## Account & Technical Support

- Reset password: use **Forgot password** on the login page; link expires in 1 hour.
- App requires iOS 15+ or Android 10+.
- Clear app cache: Settings → Storage → Clear cache (does not delete orders).
- For login loops, try incognito mode or a different browser first.

## Contact & Escalation

- Live chat hours: Mon–Fri 9am–6pm ET.
- Email: support@acmeshop.example (24–48 hr response).
- Say **"speak to a human"** anytime to open a priority ticket.
