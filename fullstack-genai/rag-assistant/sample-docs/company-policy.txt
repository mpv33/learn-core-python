# Company Knowledge Base — Sample Document for RAG testing

## Vacation Policy

All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per year.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.
Unused vacation days roll over up to 5 days into the next calendar year.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local timezone.
Equipment stipend of $500 is provided for home office setup.

## Refund Policy

Customer refunds are processed within 7 business days for eligible purchases.
Digital products may be refunded within 14 days if less than 2 hours of usage.
Contact support@company.com with your order ID to initiate a refund.

## Security Guidelines

All employees must enable two-factor authentication on company accounts.
Do not share credentials or store passwords in plain text.
Report security incidents to security@company.com within 1 hour of discovery.
